What is Robofly?

A flying platform inspired by nature.

Instead of relying on the usual exposed propeller look, Robofly uses butterfly-inspired flapping wings to create a friendlier and more memorable short-range imaging experience.

🦋

Biomimetic Flight

Butterfly-inspired flapping-wing motion creates an organic and visually distinctive flying identity.

🛡️

Approachable Design

Robofly avoids the intimidating exposed-propeller appearance of many conventional drones.

📷

Short-Range Imaging

Built with an onboard Raspberry Pi camera for close-range visual capture and demonstrations.

✨

Showcase Appeal

Designed for exhibitions, STEM showcases, branded events and audience-facing demonstrations.

Built to Demonstrate

From concept to prototype.

Robofly combines a lightweight polymer wing membrane, carbon-fibre wing supports, a servo-driven flapping mechanism, a 3D-printed body and an onboard camera system.

This section gives investors proof that Robofly is not just a visual idea. It is a real prototype with physical design and engineering work behind it.

Why buy Robofly?

Not just another drone.

Robofly does not need to outperform every quadcopter in every use case. Its value is different: design, novelty, approachability and audience experience.

Conventional Drone

  • Usually relies on exposed rotary propellers.
  • Often looks mechanical, common and less emotionally engaging.
  • Competes mainly through speed, payload, range and stability.
  • May feel less approachable in indoor or people-facing spaces.

Robofly

  • Uses butterfly-inspired flapping wings.
  • Looks friendly, memorable and booth-attracting.
  • Designed for education, exhibitions and branded experiences.
  • Creates a differentiated niche inside the unmanned aircraft market.
